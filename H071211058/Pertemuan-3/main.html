NWJNS DEBUT

On July 1, the first teaser videos were released and it was also announced that the group would release their first content on the 22nd. On July 22, the group released the debut music video for the track "Attention" and the debut schedule for their first EP, New Jeans. Teaser photos were released that same day.[10] The music videos for "Hype Boy" and "Hurt" were released on July 23 and July 25, respectively.

On August 1, the group's debut EP was released digitally, along with the music video for "Cookie." NewJeans then held their debut stage on the August 4 broadcast of Mnet's M! Countdown, performing all their three lead singles, "Attention", "Hype Boy", and "Cookie."[12] On August 18, the group took their first-ever music show win on the August 18 broadcast of M! Countdown.
